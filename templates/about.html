About

This site was inspired by my mother. She always wanted to share recipes with my aunts. Someone in the family would always have a "secret" recipe for something. Whether the recipe be for Spare ribs or cheese cake, those recipes would be hunted down. The time it took to organise a meet up with other family members would make the task tedious and difficult. So whenever there was a meet up my mom would run to the kitchen with my aunts and and spend the entire day writing down recipes. The collection of custom recipe books filled the kitchen cabinets and would take up a lot of space.

Thus 5 Minute Foods was born. I created this site so that no matter where you are in the world, finding and sharing your recipe with people would not be difficult. You might find something that surprises you or something that takes you home. May your table always be filled with friends and family.

Happy Cooking - Keis
